What is On-Page SEO?

On page Search Engine Optimization is used to optimize an individual web-page. This comes to the help with higher search ranking. This also results in more qualified traffic. The key elements include:
1. Now let us take a look at how you can optimize your Title Tags: These are the titles of the page which provide details to search engines about what this particular page is all about. This title must be visible with the primary keyword included.
2. Meta Descriptions: These are summaries that come under the title in search results. Page titles should be descriptive and contain the keyword.
3. Quick tips on Headings (heading 1, heading 2 etc.) - The title mentions about your content. It makes them more readable, and helps search engines understand your page.
4. Quality of content: The contents are provided with unique, valuable and good quality. It has to be relevant and naturally fit lots of different keywords.
5. Optioning of Keywords: Naturally keywords will be pointed in content. They should also be present in the title, headings and meta descriptions as well.
6. Linking to Other Pages on Your Site (Internal Linking): Helps search engines understand the structure of your website. It also helps a lot to retain the users on your website.
7. Images should be pertinent and of high quality-Image Optimization. Don't forget to use alt text with appropriate keywords. So that search engines will be able to understand them.
8. URL: Clearly Maintain the main keyword within it. The purpose is to help search engines and users better understand what the page content depicts.
